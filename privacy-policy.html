AaronSlayer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

What is AaronSlayer?

AaronSlayer is an internal Google Sheets add-on that pulls advertising performance data from ad platforms (Google Ads, GA4, Facebook Ads, TikTok Ads, Microsoft Ads, Pinterest Ads) into Google Sheets for reporting purposes.

What data we access

When you connect your ad account, AaronSlayer accesses read-only advertising data such as impressions, clicks, spend, and campaign information. We only access the data you explicitly request through the add-on.

How data is used

Your advertising data is written directly into your Google Sheet. It is not sent to any external server, stored in any database, or processed outside of Google Sheets.

Data storage

OAuth tokens (used to authenticate with ad platforms) are stored securely in Google Apps Script's built-in property storage, tied to your Google account. No credentials or tokens are stored outside of Google's infrastructure.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your data stays in your Google Sheet.

Internal use only

This tool is built for internal team use at The Speed Agency. It is not a commercial product and is not offered to the general public.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact your team administrator.
